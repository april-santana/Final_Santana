Covid-19 Hospitalizations in New York State from March 2020 to April 2020

Hospitals experienced a surge of cases at the start of the pandemic. New York state was hit the worst by the first wave and many hospitals were overwhelmed. The age demographic of 65 and older made up the majority of hospitalizations. Many counties - Bronx and Kings - had a surplus in patients.
